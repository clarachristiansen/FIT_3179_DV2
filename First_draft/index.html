Understanding Global Warming: Causes, Consequences, and Solutions

Climate change is a critical global issue caused by the significant increase in greenhouse gas emissions, especially carbon dioxide (CO2), in the past century. Human activities like fossil fuel burning, deforestation, industry, and agriculture release massive CO2 and other greenhouse gases into the atmosphere. These gases trap heat, resulting in a "greenhouse effect" and a gradual rise in Earth's average temperature. This disrupts natural climate patterns, leading to more severe weather events, rising sea levels, ecosystem changes, and food and water security threats. Addressing climate change requires collective efforts like cleaner energy, sustainable land use, and conservation. Throughout this page you will gain insights about the causes, consequences and solutions associated with climate change.
